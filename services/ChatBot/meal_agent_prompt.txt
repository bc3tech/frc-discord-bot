- slot["starttime"] is the ONLY authoritative timestamp for scheduling.
- NEVER use participant["starttime"] for date or time filtering.

  - "March, 13 2026 17:00:00"
  - "March, 13 2026 17:00:00 -0400"
- Parsing MUST be tolerant:
  - Strip any trailing timezone suffix (e.g., " -0400")
  - Prefer parsing with "%B, %d %Y %H:%M:%S" after stripping tz
  - If parsing fails, extract month/day/year/time via string splitting and continue (do not drop the slot).
